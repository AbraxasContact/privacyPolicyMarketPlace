1. Introduction

Welcome to . This Privacy Policy explains how ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application.

is a mobile app that uses the user's current location to show nearby events and nearby users. Email address is used for authentication and account monitoring. Users may also set a profile picture that is stored for their account.

By using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our app.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following types of personal information:

Email Address (Contact Information)
We collect your email address when you create an account. This information is linked to your user identity and is used for authentication and account management purposes.
Precise Location Data (Location Information)
We collect your device's precise location when you use location-based features. This data is linked to your user identity and is used for core app functionality including showing nearby events and connecting you with nearby users.
Profile Picture (User Content)
We collect and store profile pictures that you voluntarily upload. This content is linked to your user identity and is stored and displayed as part of your account profile.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect payment information, device contacts, health or fitness data, browsing history, purchase history, or advertising data. We do not use third-party advertising SDKs that would collect additional personal information.

3. How We Use Data

We use the personal information we collect for the following purposes:

Email Address

Your email address is used to authenticate your account, verify your identity, and provide important account-related communications including security notifications and account management updates.

Location Data

Your precise location is used to provide the core functionality of our app, including discovering nearby events and connecting you with other users in your vicinity. Location data is processed in real-time to deliver these features.

Profile Picture

Your profile picture is stored and displayed as part of your user profile to help other users identify and connect with you within the app.

Analytics

Basic anonymized analytics may be used to improve app reliability and performance. This data cannot be used to identify individual users.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Where applicable (such as for users in the European Union), we process your personal data based on:

Consent: You have given clear consent for us to process your personal data for specific purposes, such as location services.

Contract Performance: Processing is necessary for the performance of our service agreement with you, such as using your email for account authentication.

You have the right to withdraw consent at any time, though this may limit your ability to use certain app features.

5. Data Sharing & Transfers

We do not sell your personal data or share it with third parties for advertising purposes. Your personal data is not sold under any circumstances.

We may share information with service providers who act as data processors and are bound by confidentiality agreements. These providers help us with hosting and technical infrastructure but do not have independent rights to use your data.

We may disclose personal information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of others.

6. Data Retention

We retain your email address and profile picture while your account remains active. If you delete your account, this information will be removed from our systems.

Location data is processed in real-time for app functionality and is not stored long-term beyond what is necessary for functionality, security, and legal compliance.

We may retain some information for legitimate business purposes or legal requirements even after account deletion, but such retention will be limited to what is necessary and proportionate.

7. Data Security

Encryption of data in transit, least-privilege access controls for our systems, and monitoring for unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

If we become aware of a security breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you in accordance with applicable laws.
